India is about to complete 75 years as an independent nation.


Looking back, our history seems shrouded

in clouds of both hope and conflict.

Today, we have a generation who was not even born when the guiding principles of this society were forged and articulated in our Constitution, including the Preamble.


How many of this generation, and even the older one, agree with these guiding principles?


Which priorities, in this landscape riven by inequality and climate insecurity, do we wish to work toward?


What do we imagine for tomorrow, dare we say, hope?
